Singapore's Attractions

Botanic Gardens

Singapore's first UNESCO World Heritage site, the Botanic Gardens is one of Singapore's major attractions. It contains some of Singapore's wilder heritage and it's greenery is a sight to behold!

Chinatown

Singapore's version of Chinatown has many things that will bring you a taste of Chinese heritage. From authentic Chinese delicacies to the bright red lanterns, there's never a dull moment exploring the neighbourhood.

Clarke Quay

One of Singapore's older destinations, Clarke Quay is a historical riverside quay situated around Singapore River that contains many attractions such as a fun nightlife scene and amazing scenery for tourists and locals alike.
